1. Breaststroke: “The Frog Kick”

Body flat, scoop arms, frog kick, breathe when arms scoop.

Breaststroke Tutorial

2. Freestyle: “Front Crawl”

Reach one arm, kick from hips, breathe side every few strokes.

Freestyle Tutorial

3. Butterfly: “The Dolphin Stroke”

Wave-like motion, both arms swing, dolphin kick, kick-pull-breathe rhythm.

Butterfly Tutorial

4. Backstroke: “Float and Paddle”

Lie on back, windmill arms, gentle kicks, breathe normally.

Backstroke Tutorial
